Lash extensions are a little beauty miracle – wake up in the morning and already look perfect. To make the look last, keep a few things in mind.

## The first 24 hours

In the first 24 hours the glue needs to fully cure. Avoid water, steam (sauna!) and rubbing your eyes.

## Cleanse gently

Clean your lashes regularly with a mild, oil-free foam. Oils dissolve the glue and shorten the hold.

## Hands off plucking

As tempting as it is – never pull on the extensions. It also damages your natural lashes.

## Regular refills

A refill every 2–3 weeks is recommended to keep the full look. Best to book your appointment in advance.
