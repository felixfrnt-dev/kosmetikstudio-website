Microneedling is one of the most popular anti-aging treatments – and rightly so. But what actually happens during it?

## The principle

Very fine needles create tiny, controlled micro-channels in the skin. These stimulate the body's own regeneration: the skin produces more collagen and elastin.

## The benefits

- Finer, more even skin texture
- Reduction of fine lines
- Improved absorption of active ingredients
- A more radiant complexion

## Who is it for?

Microneedling suits almost every skin type – especially with first fine lines, enlarged pores or dull skin. In a personal consultation we'll determine whether the treatment is right for you.

> Tip: For an optimal result we recommend a course of several sessions a few weeks apart.
