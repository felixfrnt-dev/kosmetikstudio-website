The cold season is a real challenge for our skin. Dry heating air, icy wind and temperature swings draw out moisture – leading to tightness, redness and a dull complexion.

## 1. Choose richer care

In winter, a little more is good: switch from a light gel texture to a richer cream with nourishing lipids. This strengthens the skin barrier and holds moisture longer.

## 2. Cleanse gently

Avoid harsh, drying cleansers. A mild, pH-neutral cleanse protects your skin's natural protective film.

## 3. Hydrate from within

Even in winter: drink plenty! Unsweetened teas and water noticeably support your skin's moisture balance.
